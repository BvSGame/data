# Draft 11, Part 2/2 - Mirror, Mirror on the Wall

[[April 17, 2026]]

R: I guess that’s what I won.
C: No. You’re wrong.

TV turns on by itself. We see some image of a female person walking in some sort of palace. She’s dressed in rich long dress, her movements are smooth and precise, her heels are clicking way too expressively.

R: Wait. Charley. Am I hallucinating?
C: No. I see the same thing. Unless…
R: Unless what?
C: Unless we share the same hallucinations. Is it even possible?
R: (shrugs) It’s a big universe…
J (VO): (tapping the glass from inside) Is this thing on? One, two, three. Can you hear me?
R: That TV was broken.
C: I remember that.
J (VO): For now, I’ll read aloud the testing thing. Just to see how the transmission goes. Two, oh, two, four, brown…
R: We were going to fix it later but we had no reason to bother.
C: Unless you decide to retire from time travel and watch the news 24/7.
Both: Never happens.
J (VO): Oh but this is good news now.
R: Charlotte…
C: Yes?…
R: Don’t you have a… weird feeling?
C: You mean… like we’re…
R: …being watched.
C: Live…
J (VO): Indeed. Breaking news: drop the drama, I hear you.
R: Oh yeah? Why should we believe it?
C: Skeptic till the end of times, aren’t you?
J (VO): You shouldn’t believe it. You should accept it. Man, what a love story you had. Broken Rick breaks Saylor because she wanted to fix broken Rick.
R: You’re not invited for this talk! Mind your business, whoever you are!!
C: (quietly) Ricky, calm down, you’re shouting at the telly.
J (VO): Oh, my business? I do mind my business. In fact, it was my business. Or maybe… I was business.
R: Drop the mystery, I’m not buying it.
J (VO): Don’t you think we share something in common with Saylor? I mean, I think my face gives it away. Or maybe it’s my height. Or maybe my body shape.
C: It can’t be possible…
R: You mean…
C: I’m afraid she is.
R: Are we having the same thought?
C: Yes. We do. (to @J) But you’re merely a performer!
J (VO): Ha. I think it’s much more complicated than that. Well, I’ll leave some food for thought to you. Bon appetit. Oh and, I'm Jenesis.

TV switches off.

C: Agency trickery…
R: No. Certainly not.
C: How do you know?
R: I know Saylor too well. This… felt like a mirror version of her. The mirror is warped, of course, but… It felt too real.
C: Your instincts… One minute you’re oblivious, next minute you see a glitch in the matrix. I want to borrow your brain just to understand how it feels like.
R: My brain stays with me, thank you very much. And if you ever decide to time travel to learn human brain transplantation, I won’t consent for being your patient.
C: Sometimes you’re fountaining brilliant ideas. How didn’t I think of it? I’ll consider that.
R: Damn…
C: Remember the verbal agreement? You're not leaving me alone. So? (holds out her hand)
R: (resistantly) No.
C: Okay.

@C swiftly puts her arm around @R's shoulders, and the second before she activates the vortex manipulator...

R: No!!

Zap.
