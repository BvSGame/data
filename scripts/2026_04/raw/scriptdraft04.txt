# Draft 4 - The Cold Star

[[April 8, 2026]]

M: Sasha? What’s the matter?
S: Uh. Marie, hi. Eheh.
M: Sasha. You can breathe. Slowly. Right? Breathe in, breathe out. Keep practicing.
S: (breathes in and out) I want to see Saylor.
M: Not again…
S: But we can do this!
M: Do what?
S: Bring her here!
M: She is not a doll, y’know.
S: I know she’s a living person. But remember what we are.
M: Top secret organization. The government-funded agency. And we don’t do kidnapping of idols! Heh, regrettable pretty much.
S: Oh I know that spark in your eyes.
M: Spark? Don’t be ridiculous.
S: No, I mean it! I know you want to do it yourself!
M: Wh-- Are you breaking into my own intranet accounts now?!
S: No-no-no, I mean, it’s in your eyes. You’re like an open book.
M: Funny. Never thought my extroversion would be that exposing. Well, if you’re gonna be my tail for the rest of afternoon, then follow me.
S: Are you… giving a permission?
M: You won’t let me go otherwise. I know you too well.

@M and @S enter @M’s workspace and sit at the computer desk.

M: So! You wanna see Saylor? There she is! Our “stellar,” as she often says, time hopper, but she’s here at the moment. Parked to take a breather. But if I were you, I would be careful. And believe me, I were you, once.
S: You’re talking in riddles.
M: What year are we in?
S: 2024?
M: I know Saylor since we were 15. Since 2018. I remember I was a huge fan of her. She was magnificent.
S: High grades. The winner of Science Olympiad. Once popular and charismatic.
M: And cold.
S: Sorry?
M: She is cold. She didn’t need those achievements. She didn’t care.
S: How do you know?
M: Well. Here comes a trick. See that guy near her?
S: Well?
M: Rick. Her childhood friend. He and Saylor have a platonic sibling relationship for many years because he suffered a trauma after a car crash where he lost the family when he was 8.
S: Damn. That hits hard.
M: There’s a time and place for bad jokes, Sasha.
S: I didn’t mean it like that. Poor soul.
M: Enough sentiments.
S: So what’s the trick?
M: Ah. I was clever. I and Rick were dating. At 15.
S: What?
M: Yeah. That was my way to get closer to the star. Only to realize that star wasn’t generating heat I wanted. And here he is…
S: A broken guy.
M: The only person I genuinely care about. We may have disbanded, but I have every intention to get him back. So… I might as well just help you.
S: Y’know, you don’t have to tell me. It’s a known fact that you’re obsessing over him. You think I wouldn’t notice the logs? How you circumvent online privacy measures to collect data about everyone you put a target on?
M: I begin to hate being an open book… There needs to be some piece of privacy even for me.
S: Well, excuse me, but you’re the one using the agency’s resources for personal needs.
M: That’s not personal! You don’t get it.
S: Keep lying.
M: Hmph. If you want Saylor, I’ll get her for you. But for goodness sake, don’t ever comment on Rick’s situation. I’ll handle it personally. It takes some… delicacy.
S: Huh. Alright.
