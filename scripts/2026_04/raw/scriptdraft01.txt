# Draft 1 - The Victorian Storm

[[April 7, 2026]]

Victorian time period. We see @S (tech student from 2024) and @T (sharpshooter girl from Wild West). They're dressed to blend in with the time period. @S managed to find a blonde wig that hides her pink hair partially colored blue and violet.

C: Hey you! Fellow time travelers!
T: (confused, slightly startled) What?
C: Wait for me!

@S and @T are approached by a young woman dressed in Victorian clothes, carrying a parasol. @C.

C: You didn't notice I'm standing out from the crowd.
S: Well, you look casual to me.
C: Oh. Really? Thank you! I thought I wouldn't blend in.
T: Saylor, don't you feel some weirdness in the air?
S: I do... As usual, your instincts are spot on...
C: This look... I know where it goes...
S: Fellow time travelers? How do you know?
C: I'm just like you. And I spot the quirks of time travelers quite easily.
T: You want us to believe that?
S: Toni, let's uh, be more welcoming.
T: Hah. You tell me.
S: Who are you?
C: I'm Charlotte.
S: Nice to meet you, Charlotte. I'm Saylor and this is Toni.
T: Saylor!
C: But these are not the names you used to introduce yourselves. Ah. Don't tell me. You're incognito.
S: Of course! And if you really are a time traveler, I would advise to keep the real names secret.
T: Saylor, you're trusting a complete stranger!
S: She trusted us her real name first. That's a fair exchange, don't you think?
T: Whatever.
C: Actually, I've seen you before. In other places. But never talked to you. This is the first time we meet. But we'll meet again.
S: Wait, I don't get it. First time, but you've seen us before?
C: Well, I saw my future self walking with you two and some other guys, as if we were a team. Of course, I know the rule against crossing one's own timeline, so I couldn't approach you.
S: This isn't helping.
T: Who are other guys you saw? Can you describe them?
C: One is a male, he has long-ish brown hair. And another is a girl, shoulder-length blonde with sleeveless shirt.
T: Rick and Marie...
C: Pardon?
T: Ah, never mind. Saylor, she indeed is a time traveler. There's no other way she would see our friends, they haven't arrived at this period just yet.
S: Okay, Toni, I take your point. So, why are you here?
C: There should be a reason? I'm just traveling for fun! Seeing one thing after the next.
T: And how do you travel?

@C rolls up her sleeve to show some gadget on her forearm.

C: This. Pretty complicated arm brace. Vortex manipulator. Also, this parasol.
T: Parasol?
C: It's multi-functional, but mainly it helps finding a power source for my next trip.
T: This is mad.
C: Well. I'm not complaining.
S: You will. Once this adventure with your vortex manipulator turns into a meat grinder.
T: Ew, Saylor, that's horrible.
C: It worked flawlessly for years.
S: And what if it fails?
C: I believe, when your machine develops a fault, you're horrified even more.
S: Well. That's... not good indeed.
T: Okay, cease the talk, someone is coming.
