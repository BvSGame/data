# Draft 8, Part 1/2 - One-Second Card

[[April 11, 2026]]

2024. SAYLOR’s temporary house. @C, still dressed in 1980s fashion, is sitting at the table in the kitchen, sipping tea. Now, say hi to @M! She enters the house.

C: Marie! There you are!
M: Oh. So 1980s is trending now, isn’t it?
C: Ha-ha. Well. For now. You know how our fashion works. Today it’s 1980s, tomorrow it’s Stone Age, and the day after it’s 14th Millennium. And it’s cyclical. All of that while tea in 2024 stays hot. Sit down.

@M sits down at the table across from @C. @C pours some tea for @M.

M: So I wasn’t wrong when I found this whole deal confusing…
C: One gets used to it pretty quickly. Look at Toni.
M: Well, at the moment I’d like to know where Rick is.
C: Ask Saylor. She knows.
M: And where is she?
C: Went for a stroll. She is lecturing Alice on how to relax and watch birds sing. But Alice always wants to blow something up. That’s a typical explosives expert for you.
M: Ha-ha. Typical indeed.
C: I don’t like the image of a pigeon with a small stick of dynamite in its claws.
M: Listen… You’re traveling with them for a while, aren’t you?
C: Well, yeah. What is it, our 11th or 12th meeting maybe.
M: I can only take your word for it. One moment you’re here, next moment you’re gone, and flash, you reappear.
C: Out of sequence…
M: Out of sequence?
C: Yeah. It’s not a straight line for me. I almost expect next version of Saylor not to remember my phone gadget because it happens in her future. To be honest, I would be happier to stick to just one version of you lot. It feels too much…
M: So why don’t you?
C: Marie. I’m an independent time traveler. It’s in my DNA. You guys are nice, but my device allows for snappier hopping. More maneuverability. I would see 40 time periods while you’re dragging your feet on 1.
M: Must be one hell of a wardrobe you have.
C: Ha-ha, well. What’s true is true. Several wardrobes, in fact.
M: H-how?
C: You may say I’m pretty popular in a few places. Enough to have rooms of my own.
M: Are you a performer or something?
C: Of course I am! And it helps. You dance, you crack a standup joke, you cosplay, and boom! People provide you some extra space, or you can afford it.
M: Popular… That’s something Saylor turned down back in the day…
C: I know. She told me that.
M: What a missed opportunity…
C: Any person is free to make a choice of their own.
M: I suppose you’re right. I just hate seeing her tossed around here and there. She could settle down! She could have everything she wanted!
C: She chose the wind of adventure. She likes to be ascetic.
M: It’s messy and deadly.
C: But it’s certainly not static.
M: Well. There is logic in your words, I guess. Hm… Say. What do you think of… Rick?
C: Oh, not that old song again…
M: No, but I’m genuinely interested.
C: I know you are. But don’t worry about me. I’m not your competitor. Romance is just… incompatible with time travel, so it’s not my cup of tea. Just imagine the lineage, it’s gonna be hellacious.

@C gets up, she’s heading towards the door.

C: Well um, sorry, I need to go. It’s been a pleasure.
M: Wait!
C: Hm?
M: If you return, you probably will arrive too early or too late. Here, apply this special card to your vortex manipulator. Once you zap away, this card records the moment of your departure. So ultimately, from my perspective, you’ll return one second later.
C: Where did you get it?
M: The power of Agency. If only you saw our gadgetry. You have to visit it one day. But don’t tell anyone I gave this card to you. Just keep it and return to this point in time safely.
C: Okay, thank you so much!
M: Good luck.
C: Well. Gonna see this town for a while before I vanish.
M: See you in a… however long it takes for you.
