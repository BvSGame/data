# Theses
## Protagonist
- From the beginning of BvS1, it's known that the protagonist is an adventurous 21-year old cis female and her supposedly original name is Saylor. But her real name is Luna, much to her own surprise (not revealed until arriving in the Cube-Simulator), and there are documental proofs of it.
- Saylor would switch her name during travels, because she's very concerned about her impact on the timelines and she wants to stay incognito.
    - Darkrock knows her real name (Luna).
    - In one occasion, Saylor would have a name Stella, similar to her catchphrase.
    - In 2345, she'll use the name Jenesis. Jen for short.
    - Other variants are: Camille, Candace, Galilea, Lillie, Mackenzie, Maria, Mercy, Monica, Paulina, Violet.
- Protagonist's catchphrase is: "Stellar!" She'll use it anywhere, to the point that it gets significantly easier for some characters to track her in time and space, ruining her goal of staying incognito.
