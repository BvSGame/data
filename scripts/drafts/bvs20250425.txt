Godlike Entity

GE may act differently on every occurrence. It takes different personality traits and it can take any visual appearance.

Normally GE appears when the valuable characters die or a critical event happens. On the “Game Over” screen (when sleepwalkers manage to enter the house and steal the fridge), there are two choices given: restart the game or see what happens next. When the player chooses the latter, they can see some moments after stealing the fridge, attempts of resistance, consequences, and then GE appears to offer the player a return to the stable point in the game.
