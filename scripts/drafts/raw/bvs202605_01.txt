# Draft 1

[[May 2, 2026]]

## Part 1

Spaceship in the 27th century. We see two girls walking. @S from 2024, who wears denim overall dress, which is an interesting match to her peach-pink beachy bob with blue and purple highlights. And @L, originally a virtual assistant from an unknown time period, who found her new home in @S’s gadgets some time ago, but during time travels, she went to 2345 to build herself a realistically human physical body. @L wears a mix of a schoolgirl and astronomy professor\: velvet jacket, white shirt, green neck bow, pitch black hair with slight red tint, ponytail with green bow, pleated skirt, stockings and heeled shoes. @L is taller than @S. 

S: So. This spaceship is strange… No, this whole mission is strange… We nearly got killed, and you’re so confident in telling me you know what you’re doing that--

A door closes behind them.

S: --Lisa!
L: Don’t even try to use the terminal. I encrypted all input.
S: What the hell are you doing?! Allow me to continue my investigation!

@L grabs @S’s arm and takes her to the escape pod.

L: No, Saylor. Leave it to me. I must proceed without your presence. I’m risking less than you. If I get destroyed, I go and build a new body if I want to. If you get destroyed… you die.
S: I choose to take the risks! Hands off me! Ow!!
L: Out of my way.
S: Lisa! You’re making a grave mistake! Stop it! This is not funny!
L: Be silent! This ship may be deserted, but I don’t need you to raise enough noise to invite unnecessary guests.
S: Okay. If you put me into an escape pod, I’m not leaving alone. You must go with me.
L: Dream of it.

@L shoves @S into the escape pod.

S: No, Lisa, please! Keep me here! Don’t eject me!
L: I’m not listening to you.
S: Get me out of here. This is an order!
L: We’re partners, Saylor. You can’t give me orders.
S: This is not a partnership when you throw your partner out into an open space because you’ve calculated your own plan!
L: It is.
S: No! No!!! Don’t do it!

An escape pod with @S in it gets ejected into space.

S: No-o-o!!!
L: I apologize.

Then @L turns away and walks to the closed door, opening it.

We see escape pod aimlessly floating in space, distancing from the ship further and further and further away…

## Part 2

In the escape pod. @S tries to communicate with @L, but there’s no signal, no contact.

S: Lisa! LISA!! COME ON!!!

@S, frustrated, kicks some wall in the pod, sits down. Head in hands.

Then in a few seconds she comes to the realization. She begins talking to herself, speedrunning through the thoughts.

S: Wait a minute… This… this isn’t just a pod… (delightful expression of slow realization) Of course! How could I forget the construction of this thing? Okay, there must be some emergency panel somewhere… (repeatedly snapping the fingers and looking around) Where is it, where is it, where is it?… (checks the pockets) I need a scr-- (finds a screwdriver) Right.

In some wall, @S finds a way to remove some cover plate. Then, she finds a small door. She opens it and sees a set of buttons, toggle switches, dials and wires.

S: (laughs) Control panel!!

@S tries some combinations, and one of the combination fires up the small engines.

S: Alrighty. Time to make a comeback. (pulls out a tablet from her handbag, plugs it into the port in control panel, does some frantic tapping) Now we target the ship and use the prewritten script for hacking the entrance… Lisa must be relaxed and lowered her guard at this point… Clever.

The escape pod is now targeted. @S braces for kickstarting the power boost.

S: (closes eyes, deeply breathes in and out, opens eyes) Fire.

@S presses the button on the control panel. Escape pod begins shaking a bit and fly towards the opening entrance. @S tries to hold onto something during the flight.

S: Ha-ha-a-a-a!!! Stellar!!!

Now, escape pod gets back into the ship. @S enters the ship and walks in the corridors.

Shortly, @L finds her, but she doesn’t turn around.

L: You’re too desperate to test your mortality.
S: It’s better to do it here than passing the same test being suffocated in deep space. You really think you can get rid of me and get away with that?
L: Saylor. This is a dangerous mission.
S: I don’t care! We go together. Don’t try any tricks on me. You’ve just wasted my resources for forgiveness, they are extremely limited right now.
L: Sigh… Well. Let’s go forward.
S: (pats @L on the shoulder) Good girl.

They both keep going further.
