# Draft 3 - Housewife 1\.0

[[May 2, 2026]]

2024. @S’s rental house.

We only see @L cooking something in the kitchen. She’s wearing apron over her “professor-student” outfit.

We hear a muffled voice of @S shouting behind the front door.

S: Damn it!!

@L hears it and walks to the front door. @S rings the doorbell.

L: Coming!

@L opens the front door.

S: I’m an idiot. Forgot the keys at the university-- (notices an apron) Oh.
L: Good afternoon, Saylor!

@L greets @S with a kiss, first on one cheek, then on the other. @S is stunned.

S: Okay. That’s new.
L: Something wrong?
S: Uh. No. (staring at the apron) Nothing at all.
L: Well, don’t stand there. Come in.

@L goes back to the kitchen. @S enters slowly, feeling some shock. Her shoulders are uneasy.

S: Software update?
L: What?
S: Something’s different.
L: What do you mean?
S: Memory wipe as well?
L: I don’t understand what you’re talking about.
S: Since when are you all… housewifey?
L: Wha-- No! Human! Just trying something once in a while. Why can’t I?
S: Interesting. So you’re now kissing and cooking.
L: Well. (looking at what she does with the dishes, almost like she’s double-checking her own behavior) I think so.

@S joins @L in the kitchen.

L: I’m not entirely sure about kissing though, can I try it again? (reaches out to get closer, but @S softly pushes her away)
S: No-no-no, he-heh, you did just fine for the first time.
L: I’m sorry for inconvenience.
S: This is not how you wear apron, y’know.
L: I did it right. See? It’s front-facing, neck, left arm, right arm--
S: No. You’re still wearing your costume.
L: So?
S: You need to change. You’ll get it all splattered.
L: I’m not worried about it.
S: (laughs) You’re wearing expensive clothing. Just judging by the velvet. You can’t be serious.
L: (pauses) Expensive?
S: Yes. It doesn’t look cheap for sure. How much did it cost?
L: Um. I don’t know.
S: What do you mean, you don’t know? You’ve got it somewhere, after all.
L: Um… There is a tiny little moment…
S: What is it?
L: (whispering to @S’s ear) I haven’t paid for it. (looks at @S and grins)
S: You crazy woman…
L: I’m not complaining.
S: You stole it.
L: No. Nobody needed it, I took it.
S: From where?
L: 2345, where I was built. Some doll was dressed, and clearly it didn’t need those clothes.
S: So you stole it.
L: This is not stealing! Dolls own nothing!
S: And you’re not a doll then.
L: I’m more and beyond. (showing a dish with what she’s been cooking) Human plus. (keeps cooking)
S: But you were created in a doll factory.
L: So what? I have non-doll brains. You know that.
S: Okay. Never mind. So, cooking? Since when?
L: Why not? I enjoy the process! Wanna be my taster?
S: I’m tempted but wary.
L: Don’t worry, it’s not poisonous.
S: (worried laugh) Y-yeah.
L: Well, I’ve triple-checked all the expiration dates for every ingredient, and I’ve scanned the quality myself, but I’m not so sure about mixing.
S: Well, at least it doesn’t look bad. Must be tolerable at worst.
L: Recipes are a bit of a blind spot, but I promise to glimpse a cookbook.
S: Hm. Actually, to hell with this. Let’s do it.
