# Draft 2 - Cornered

[[April 8, 2026]]

There's a scene where sleepwalkers are surrounding Charlotte and she has nowhere to go.

C: Oh. Nice. You've cornered me.

The sleepwalkers, glitching as usual, slowly keep getting closer.

C: You've actually, truly cornered me. That's a neat wall, it's beautifully smooth and firm.

And closer.

C: Here's a rare moment of honesty for you: I don't like dying!

And closer. @C is noticeably panicking.

C: Oh, I suppose you're not listening. Well, you'll listen to this, better record it. Funny story, I use this umbrella to charge from raw electricity like thunderbolts. That's how I travel. But that's about collection. What about... deployment?

The sleepwalkers are close enough to reach her.

C: (deep breath, collects herself) Gentlemen, watch and learn. One word...

@C snaps the parasol.

C: Zap.

Sleepwalkers are getting heavily electrocuted from bolts parasol throws. Bolts are even passing from one sleepwalker to another. Their chest units are exploding and they fall without further movements. @C folds her parasol and pokes lying sleepwalkers with it, to make sure they're defeated.

C: Well. I said. I don't like dying. Seeing you dying also counts. Because... you make too much noise when you fall. (looking at the folded parasol) Sigh... Discharged. What a whacker. Oh well. Gotta find another thunderbolt.

@C walks away.
