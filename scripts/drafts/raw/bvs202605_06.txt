# Draft 6 - I, Human

[[May 4, 2026]]

2024 streets.

We see a childhood friend of @S’s, @R. Fellow tech student. As he walks, he’s talking to @S on the phone.

R: Saylor, you’re full of bad ideas.
S (VO): Not really.

Technical university. @S is alone in the room. Well, almost.

S: I decided to stay with the Sleepwalker.

Camera moves from the view of @S to the view of the disabled Sleepwalker with disassembled chest and loose wires. Short dramatic pause.

@S connects the tablet she’s talking on to the Sleepwalker’s speakers, then she begins to fiddle. As @S and @R talk, scenes are switching\: when @S talks, we see @S, and when @R talks, we see @R.

S: Back to the roots. I was asked by the university to dig deep into a three-minute glitch mystery, I’m doing it. One good thing about it is: it complains much less than you do.
R: Because it’s not programmed to complain.
S: While you are. I’d like to file a complaint to your manufacturers. Oops! Error 404.
R: Saylor. Bad joke. Bad girl. Bringing up my car crash incident is not good.
S: You don’t feel… (crosses the wires, gets showered with sparks) ...traumatized out of the sudden. Neuroleptics?
R: It’s been 13 years ago. Why should I be all Mr\. Sad-man if I can tease my surrogate sister here and now.
S: Oh, I’m pleased. Now, you’re on a mission, brother. You need to--
R: Cover you. I know. Little Saylor is scared of a big tall doll!
S: Watch your tone. If you keep it, I’ll organize Five Nights at Saylor’s.
R: Don’t.
S: Scared? Shall I bring some nappies on my way home? Or you’ll manage to the restroom till you hear “Here’s Lisa”?
R: I’m not scared of some unhinged robogirl.
S: Oh yeah. Except she claims to be
RICK/SAYLOR: Human Plus.
S: So, no rocket science. Thank your lucky stars that she’s not a cyberman. I’d hate being Lisa from this perspective.
R: Yeesh.
S: Beat her with your conversational brilliance. I believe in you.
R: Shut up.
S: I always knew you’re one hell of a charmer.
R: Keep doing your homework.

@R hangs up and walks to the front door of @S’s rental. He knocks on the door, even if he knows about the doorbell. Deliberate.

@R listens closely. But nothing can be heard. 3 seconds. 5 seconds. Not a rustle.

He’s about to knock again. But the door opens earlier. @L makes a sudden appearance, dressed as usual, “astronomy professor in a student body.”

L: Stealth!
R: Ha. Nice job. But you’re meant to say--
L: Good afternoon, Rick!
R: Check. May I come in?
L: Be my guest!
R: (pats @L on the shoulder) You’re getting better.

Now indoors. @L closes the door.

R: I was warned about your “Greetings Pack.”

However, @L kisses @R straight on the lips.

R: But I wasn’t warned about this.
L: This was intentional. Different gender, different configuration. Well? It’s not interesting to stand here. (pushes @R) To the kitchen you go!

Kitchen! @R and @L are sitting down at the table, across from each other.

L: I prepared something consumable.

@L pushes the plate of pasta toward @R.

R: You’re kidding…
L: Something incorrect?
R: You’ve actually mastered it.
L: Is it sarcasm or sincere reaction?
R: (proceeds to eat) Lisa. You’re brilliant. This is, this is fabulous.
L: (higher pitch) You said the thing!!
R: Absolutely. You’re actually pulling off the art of the culinary very well. Saylor likes to tease you. Let me tell you, she’s wrong. Don’t listen to her.
L: Noted. (slightly lower volume, gossip tone) You really think she’s…? (whistles as in “crazy”)
R: Not to that extent, no. Have some respect.
L: Sorry. Respect. Learning respect.
R: (softly) Lisa, relax! Why are you so worried about… trying to be a little more human? It’s like your confidence plays hide and seek!

@L somehow blushes. She slightly looks away.

L: (feeling small) I… Can I be human?
R: Lisa…
L: (looking straight) Tell me honestly.
R: I… I’m not sure if I can be human, Lisa.
L: Don’t speak nonsense! You’re organic! Flesh and blood! Vulnerable to the paper cut!
R: You’re talking about physicality.
L: Sentimental as hell! Able to cry over a loss of ice cream if the context is right! Look at me. Am I even being sincere? Truly?
R: I’m afraid you’re confusing sincerity with something else here.
L: Tell me what it is. Tell me!
R: Look. Calm down. You’re capable of sincerity. I can see that. You can do honesty. You can do empathy. You can do commitment to the truth. But you still think you’re faking it. And for as long as you feel this way, you’ll be clumsy. And because of that… you’ll blame yourself for the wrong reasons.

@L gets silent. She slowly lowers her head, until her eyes are no longer visible. Feeling defeated. 5 seconds. She gasps convulsively. Sobbing?

R: You’re the best Human Plus I’ve ever seen.

@L pauses for 5 seconds. Completely frozen. Then she breaks the silence, without looking up.

L: Is pasta edible?
R: Lisa. I’ve finished it. You haven’t noticed.

Now @L slowly looks up and notices empty plate.

L: And?
R: I said.
L: (almost whispered) Fabulous.
R: Do you feel human now?

@L abruptly looks up. Eyes on @R. Light smile. Slaps the table with both hands.

L: I want to play Twister.
R: What?
L: Twister. Let’s play it.
R: Have a mercy! You’re extremely flexible and I’m extremely tired. See some imbalance?
L: Twister or I’m telling Saylor that so-called Greetings Pack went wrong and it’s been your initiative!
R: No-no, don’t!
L: (pulls out her Fab Watch and presses the button) You have 60 seconds. Tick-tock.

@R gets up immediately. As he does the thing…

L: Oh, by the way! You don’t need to worry about the homework! I’ve done it. I've cleared out your university schedule.
R: I had my homework done already.
L: Ahem, 8 days in advance.
R: How is that even possible?
L: (innocently observing her fingernails) Well, I wouldn’t advise to check one of my closets. I’ve already completed every course for you.

@R pauses, looks at @L, astonished.

L: (waves) Hello, technical specialist. My congratulations in advance!
R: You do know that *I* am supposed to study, right? How would I explain your help? “Oh I know nothing, AI 100% assisted me with my research”?
L: Allow me to be your personal assistant.
R: Interesting subversion… A professor calls herself an assistant.
L: (with pride) Very human.
