# Draft 6 - Untactical Boom

[[April 9, 2026]]

T: Is there any status update on your walking tinheads? It can’t be that dead quiet here.
S: (checking her tablet) Patience, Toni, I’m trying my best! Damn, not a blip. Uh, Charlotte, can you try?
C: This should boost the range.

@C opens her parasol and points it upwards.

S: Good, it helps.
C: But, I’ll try my software kit as well.
S: Stellar. Keep me informed.
C: (checking her device on a wrist) Hmm. Saylor, I think there’s some faint radio wave detected on Northwest. 2 o’clock. I’ll point my parasol there. Check the readings.
S: Oh. You’re not wrong. There is something. Up to 1\.25% spike every… 4-6 seconds. But I can’t figure out what it is. I don’t recognize the pattern. Toni, binoculars.
T: Okay, lemme uh… Hmm…
S: Well? Is there anything?
T: Quiet.

@T is looking into one window after the next. Until she finds…

T: Oh! A figure!
C: What is it?
T: Wa-a-a-ait… A-ha. A sleepwalker. Just one of them.
S: How distant?
T: I would say uh, 200 yards.
C: Good. Let me recalibrate. (twisting parts of the shaft) Here.
S: Spikes are rising up to 5%, it’s more steady now.
T: Can you get a remote audio capture?
S: I’m trying. The signal is still too weak. Charlotte, keep recalibrating.
C: On it. Wait!
S: What is it?
C: My sensors are detecting a rapidly moving second figure. It moves towards that sleepwalker.
S: Toni, can you see anything?
T: No, nothing.
S: Strange.
C: It’s stopped! Two points occupy the same spot.
S: What the hell is going on?
C: Now it rapidly moves back.
T: Sleepwalker isn’t moving.
S: My data makes no sense anymore. I’ll trust you two.

Explosion is heard from the distance.

T: You better trust your own ears. The sleepwalker blew up.
S: What?
C: Yeah, I saw it too. Just what’s happening?
S: Are you asking me?
A (VO): (through radio) Ah. So that’s who I saw on the roof right now. Well, your metalhead is done. Boom-whack-o-pow. Scrap metal. Mission complete, the area is clear.
S: Alice, you’re a fool! That dead sleepwalker now sends a signal for reinforcements. You’ve ruined our stealth mission! Get out of there!
A (VO): Saylor, I’m 19, not 9. I know what to do. See ya!

@A cuts off the transmission.

S: Little idiot.
T: Little? You’re 21.
C: Toni, sunshine, leave semantics for another day, alright?
T: What does semantics mean?
C: Just… keep your head clean for now, will you?
