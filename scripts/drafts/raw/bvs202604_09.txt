# Draft 9 - Ship Happens

[[April 13, 2026]]

Time machine arrives in the past. We only see the ships. It can be anything.

T: Well. For some reason, your name sounds like a job title now.
S: I hate this joke... Y'know what, actually there's something about your name too.
T: What is it?
S: It's Toni with an I. But you're behaving like Tony with a Y.
T: (laughs) Shut up.
R: Look at you two. It's like you've swapped letters between your names. Lowkey adorable, I should think.
S: Says the guy with the most average name imaginable.
R: At least I'm not showing off just by saying my name. (pauses, thrilled) Saylor. Look at the ships.
S: Rick, don't you start annoying me--
R: --No. Look. The flags.
S: So... Golden Age of Piracy... I hadn't realized. There's one problem with the time machine: it tells WHEN you go, but it doesn't tell WHERE and what the context is.
T: Are we in trouble or what?
S: Toni, your gun would be useless.
T: That's borderline offensive.
S: Borderline, yes. Because you're about to walk the plank before a deep dive.
T: What are you talking about?
S: The pirates have guns! They're armed! Also, some bombs. Which is probably neat.
T: (worried) Well. We've got to do something, I suppose.
S: At my disposal, I only have jokes about piracy.
R: Oh no...
S: Well, belt your BitTorrent clients tight!
R: Here she goes... Toni, we're in trouble earlier than you can imagine.
T: I'm in.
R: Great...
