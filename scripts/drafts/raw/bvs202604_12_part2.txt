# Draft 12, Part 2/2 - Televised Intrusion

[[April 20, 2026]]

Sudden change of the scene. @S’s temporary house, 2024.

R: Holy cow! What was it?
C: What… do you mean?
R: Saylor… Toni… Where are they?
C: On a mission.
R: Where?
C: Pripyat, 1992.
R: I know. Are they still there?
C: It’s been only a day. Chill.
R: So… Lisa tried to send Saylor alone to a mission. Because…

Flashback.

L: Saylor. There is a critically important mission.
S: Tell it.
L: But listen. You have to go alone.
S: Why?
L: I’ll assist you, and I need your undistracted expertise. This is very dangerous, and you must remain very focused. Any companion would distract you.
S: What’s the mission?
L: You go to the area that is known for its high radioactivity. But my information tells that’s not the case anymore. Someone is changing the history.
S: I’m not going alone.
L: But you must! You’re pivoting way too easily!
S: I’m taking Toni with me. It’s either me and Toni, or no one. Choose.
L: Fine. You and Toni. No others.
S: Stellar.

Back to @R and @C.

R: Uh… Er… Wait. (noticing he’s lying on sofa) I wasn’t here the last time I remember.
C: Your mind snapped.
R: What?
C: You fell asleep. You needed a nap.
R: Oh, hah. Alright. Thank you. I must’ve been really tired.
C: No wonder. Restless life of a time traveler. You have no pleasure of having a schedule.
R: I wasn’t in a coma, right?… (abruptly) Calendar!
C: What?
R: I need a calendar! What’s the day today? Quickly! My puzzle is shattered.
C: Okay, okay. It’s um, July 3rd, 2024.
R: It’s been July 2nd when we had a talk and saw TV thing.
C: Rick? You’re talking like you don’t trust the reality anymore.
R: I’m pretty confused, Charley. I almost feel like I don’t know what’s real. After all, you’re not even supposed to be here.
C: Now you’re going too far.
R: No, but think of it. Where are you even from?
C: Seriously. It doesn’t matter. I’m here, right now, listening to your ramblings. Now get up, have some tea, and put your mind together.
R: Yeah, right. But, TV thing.
C: Stop thinking too much. In your case, it’s unhealthy. Have an apple. Alright? Rick, are you listening?
R: Yeah, yeah… I’m… sorry, I’m thinking. Try to turn on that TV.
C: It’s broken.
R: No, try it.
C: Okay.

Jen: Oh, I thought you would never figure out.
C: Now we’re on the same page, Ricky. Even I am questioning the reality.
Jen: Good that you do! Because your reality is not what it’s supposed to be!
C: Are you insane?
Jen: Ah. Well. Not as insane as your beloved world. It’s messy. I hate the chaos. In any shape and form. I intend to put all things in the multiverse to the scientific order. Everything is supposed to make sense and be logical.
C: You can’t do that.
Jen: Can’t I? I only need artifacts. The more I get, the closer I am to my goal. So, your beloved Saylor must have one of those. I must remember that.
C: She surely doesn’t.
Jen: Oh yeah? The fridge. What does it contain?
C: How do you know about the fridge?
Jen: Don’t be a fool. I and Saylor had our meeting. You think I would miss that detail? Saylor can’t travel in time and space that easily, I’m on an asteroid at the moment. What is in that fridge?
C: Nobody knows. The freezer is locked, nobody could be able to open it.
Jen: Then it’s definitely an artifact.
C: What is the artifact anyway?
Jen: Oh, many of them are scattered across the universes. You probably were lucky to get one. I’m putting a big effort to hunt those down.
C: Well, good luck. Even if Saylor has it, she won’t give it to you. You’re not a trustworthy customer.
Jen: Ha! We’ll see about that.

@C turns TV off.

C: (to @R) Satisfied?
R: What a mess.
C: I know. Now hold your brains together, because we all need you collected.
R: I hope I’m not an artifact.
C: This joke is bad. But y’know, maybe you’re even right. Saylor has at least one artifact, and it might be you.
R: You mean, a literal one?
C: No! But… I don’t know. If we lose you, I’m not sure I’ll take it.
R: You’re independent. You move on.
C: Don’t. You. Dare. To say that.
R: Why do you even care?
C: Because I chose this team! Because this is the team where I feel myself a person!
