**Toni** (short for Antoinette) is a sharpshooter from [Wild West](Wild%20West.md).

| Information                       | Value                                        |
| --------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Birth date                        | 1841                                         |
| Age                               | 19                                           |
| Original location and time period | [Wild West](Wild%20West.md), [1860](1860.md) |

## Dynamics with other characters

For Toni, [Saylor](Saylor.md) is a mentor who “understands how the world works.” Eventually, Toni becomes Saylor’s girlfriend.

[Rick](Rick.md) is Toni’s best friend. Rick is the only person she can trust when Saylor does something wrong (especially morally) or goes too far.
