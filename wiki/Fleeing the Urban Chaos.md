***Fleeing the Urban Chaos*** is a musical theme associated with the [2038 mini-boss](2038%20mini-boss.md) battle. Since [Saylor](Saylor.md) herself and [Lisa](Lisa.md) as a virtual assistant appear in this battle, *Fleeing the Urban Chaos* contains reused elements of [Saylor’s Theme](Saylor%20(music).md) and [Lisa’s Theme](Lisa%20(music).md).

This track is a part of *[Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers Medley](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20Medley.md)*.
