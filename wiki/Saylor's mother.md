**[Saylor](Saylor.md)’s biological mother** is a mysterious figure. She never appears in the [franchise](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(franchise).md) but only being referred to by some characters, notably [Darkrock](Darkrock.md) and [Jenesis](Jenesis.md), seemingly having an access to [special files](Special%20files.md).

Saylor herself doesn’t even know who her mother is. Her mother got divorced when Saylor was 2 years old.
