**”Zap!”**, also **”Snap!”**, is [Charlotte](Charlotte.md)’s catchphrase.

Both variants are interchangeable:

- Charlotte says “Zap!” when she releases electricity from her [parasol](Charlotte's%20parasol.md) or before traveling in time using her [Portable Time Transporter](Portable%20Time%20Transporter.md);
- Charlotte says “Snap!” when she unfolds her parasol for any purpose.

However, catchphrase can be used out of context as well.
