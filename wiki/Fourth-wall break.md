**Fourth-wall break** is a narrative technique where characters or events go beyond the scope of fictional medium, something removes the separation/distance between viewer/player and character:

- Some character addresses the viewer/player or real-world events;
- Some character acknowledges being in the narrative or medium (game, book, video, movie, etc.);
- Some character acknowledges themself as a product of fiction;
- Some event is connected to something in reality.
