***Doki Doki Literature Club! New Heading*** (*DDLC:NH* for short) is a fan-made modification of the popular visual novel *Doki Doki Literature Club!* Modification is produced by [liledix⁴](liledix4.md) and [Xatiman](Xatiman.md).

This modification introduces [Lisa](Lisa.md), a character who also appears in [*Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers* franchise](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(franchise).md), making her a crossover character. In the lore, Lisa’s appearance in the [first *BvS* game](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(game).md) happened after events of *DDLC:NH*.
