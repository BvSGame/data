***Saylor***, also called ***Saylor’s Theme***, is a musical theme associated with the character [Saylor](Saylor.md). Since the character herself has regular appearance throughout *[Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers franchise](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(franchise).md)*, motifs of this theme are appearing in many other tracks as well.

This track is a part of *[Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers Medley](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20Medley.md)*.
