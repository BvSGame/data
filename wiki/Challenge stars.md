**Challenge stars** are a special metric of progress in the [*Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers* game series](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(franchise).md). While you can complete every level (or every most important level) to reach normal ending, challenge stars are necessary to collect in order to get a better ending.

Challenge stars allow for replayability and addition of difficulty into the established levels without implementing a difficulty setting for the whole game.

## In games

Challenge stars are prominent in the following games:

- *[Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(game).md)*
- *[Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers 2](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%202.md)*
- *[Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers 3D](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%203D.md)*

Because of the nature of the visual novel genre, there are no challenge stars in *[Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers Origins](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20Origins.md)*.

### In *BvS 1*

- [ ] #2do Reference to the level system.

In *BvS 1*, most levels have 3 “objective” challenge stars. You can choose these stars to collect, each of the stars have their objectives you need to complete to get a star.

Most levels also have 2 more stars (making it up to 5 in total per level): one is for [Hardcore Mode](Hardcore%20Mode.md) and one is for collecting a certain amount of [score points](Score%20points.md) (do not skip [Bonus Stages](Bonus%20Stage.md), because those are an easy way to get a lot of points).
