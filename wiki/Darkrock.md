**Professor Phillip Darkrock**, or simply **Darkrock**, is an [antagonist](Antagonist.md) of the [first *Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers* game](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(game).md). Subordinate of [Jenesis](Jenesis.md). One of his projects is [Cube-Simulator](Cube-Simulator.md).
