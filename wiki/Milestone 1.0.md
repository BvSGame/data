**Milestone 1.0**, also known as **2023 Reboot**, is a critical development stage in [*Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers* franchise](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(franchise).md) history, marking a breaking, [stellar](Stellar.md) change from what it has been since 2014.

2023 Reboot was driven by two factors that served as catalysts:

1. [Xatiman](Xatiman.md) introduced [liledix⁴](liledix4.md) to [*Doctor Who*](Doctor%20Who.md). As a result, liledix⁴ quickly became so engrossed in the show that he began to notice certain parallels with *Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers* and what had been missing from *BvS* all along.
2. While [liledix⁴](liledix4.md) was casually sharing details about what *Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers* was all about, he and [BlazingZephyr](BlazingZephyr.md) exchanged jokes that sparked genuine wonder: what if the project were ever to be revived?
