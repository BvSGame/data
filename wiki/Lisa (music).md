***Lisa***, also called ***Lisa’s Theme***, is a musical theme associated with the character [Lisa](Lisa.md). Originally, this track was a part of *[Doki Doki Literature Club! New Heading](Doki%20Doki%20Literature%20Club!%20New%20Heading.md)* soundtrack, and later it was remixed for [*Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers* game series](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(franchise).md), since the titular character Lisa is a crossover character.

This track is a part of *[Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers Medley](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20Medley.md)*.

## Listen

In *Medley Part 1*, *Lisa* starts playing at `2:09`.
