## Current format

```
Format  R.M.SWD.h.m
Example 0.1.514.10.4
```

| Letter(s) | Meaning                                         | In example                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **R**     | Revision of the project.                        | `0` means the project is in **zero revision**, likely indicating very early development.                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **M**     | [Milestone](Milestones.md).                     | `1` refers to **Milestone 1**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| **S**     | Stage of development in the milestone.          | `5` refers to the stage of development called **Milestone 1.5**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **W**     | Week of stage.                                  | `1` is a **second week of development** during Milestone 1.5 (because the first week starts with 0). This number resets as soon as stage of development changes. One milestone is allowed to last maximum 10 weeks of updates, even if the weeks aren’t full or some days were skipped. |
| **D**     | Day of week of stage.                           | `4` means **Tuesday**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **h**     | UTC hour, real-time.                            | `10` means **10:00 in UTC time zone**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **m**     | Minor update.<br>*Used on very rare occasions.* | `4` means the **fourth update** during 10:XX.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
