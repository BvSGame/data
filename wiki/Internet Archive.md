**Internet Archive** (also known as **Archive.org**) is a public digital library that provides free access to various media, including but not limited to music, videos, programs, games, books.

Internet Archive allows to upload and download files freely and without restrictions (as long as they don’t violate copyright).

## *Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers* assets hosted on Internet Archive

Public assets of [*Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers* franchise](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(franchise).md) can be found here: https://archive.org/details/bvsgame

Assets are free to use, they’re released under [Creative Commons](Creative%20Commons.md) license (normally [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)).
