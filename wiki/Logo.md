***Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers Logo*** (*BvS Logo*) is the most visible piece of [branding](Branding.md) for the [franchise](Bombs%20vs.%20Sleepwalkers%20(franchise).md). Logo is designed by [liledix⁴](liledix4.md).

Various versions of *BvS Logo* can be found on [Internet Archive](Internet%20Archive.md) page, particularly in this directory: https://archive.org/download/bvsgame/brand/logos/

## Known versions of *Bombs vs. Sleepwalkers Logo*

### 2023 version (current)

2023 version of *BvS Logo* is representing [2023 Reboot](Milestone%201.0.md) era of the franchise.

While it’s not significantly different from its predecessor, this is the first time when the logo used free fonts instead of commercial ones, to complement new [FLOSS](Open%20source%20and%20licensing.md) direction.

### Legacy version (2015)

Legacy version of *BvS Logo* that has been used until 2023 Reboot. It has been scrapped because of the use of commercial fonts. It’s not lost media, though.
