**”Fabulous,”** sometimes shortened to **“Fab,”** is [Lisa](Lisa.md)’s [catchphrase](Catchphrase.md).

| The What         | The Details                                                |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| First appearance | *[The Fab Watch](The%20Fab%20Watch%20(script%20draft).md)* |

## Artificial nature of the catchphrase

“Fabulous” is the first case of the catchphrase being artificially made, instead of being a natural part of the character’s vocabulary. In the script draft *[The Fab Watch](The%20Fab%20Watch%20(script%20draft).md)*, it’s been told that Lisa patched her own software to make “fabulous” her favorite word, which is sticked since then.

## Uses

- One of the Lisa’s aliases, [Dr. Elizabeth Ausbuloff](Lisa.md#Dr.%20Elizabeth%20Ausbuloff), is an anagram for “Fabulous,” with an extra F, which is explained as “frantically fabulous.”
- [Fab Watch](Fab%20Watch%20(gadget).md) has a shortened form of the catchphrase, “Fab.”
